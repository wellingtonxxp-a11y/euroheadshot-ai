FAQ

What selfie should I send?

Use a clear selfie with good lighting, no sunglasses, no heavy filters, and your face fully visible. Avoid group photos.

What is a minor revision?

Minor revisions include background tone, lighting adjustments, slightly different expression, or outfit tone. Major changes (new theme/style) require a new order.

How do I receive the final image?

We deliver directly on WhatsApp as a high-resolution image. If your package includes multiple variations, we send them together.

Can you guarantee an identical face match?

We aim for a strong resemblance, but AI can introduce small variations. We reduce this by using a clear selfie and professional parameters.
